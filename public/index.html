About Kreya IT Consulting

Salesforce Training, Consulting & Internship Programs

Kreya IT Consulting Pvt Ltd is a Salesforce-focused training and consulting practice. We work with learners at every stage — from absolute beginners to working professionals upskilling into Revenue Cloud Advanced and Agentforce — and pair every course with practical, project-based learning drawn from real client implementations.

What we offer

  • Structured live and recorded training across Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Experience Cloud, CPQ, ARM, Agentforce and Field Service
  • A dedicated student portal for secure, on-demand access to lecture recordings
  • An internship program for learners ready to work on simulated client projects
  • Implementation and configuration consulting for Salesforce Revenue Cloud Advanced

Why learners choose us

Our training is led by consultants who configure these exact Salesforce clouds for clients — so what you learn maps directly to what's expected on the job. Every track ends with a portfolio-ready project.
